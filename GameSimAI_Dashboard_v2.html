Samples from fixed distributions. Predictable. Cannot learn complex correlations between features.
LESS REALISTIC

Learns real feature correlations from 100K+ matches. Generator vs Discriminator adversarial training produces realistic players.

Key Insights
What the data told us — no one defined these
Vision beats mechanics
Vision Controller has the lowest KDA (2.1) but the highest win rate (58%). Controlling vision is a stronger predictor of winning than raw kill stats.
Gold diff at 15 → r=0.71
Gold difference at 15 minutes is the strongest single predictor of match outcome. Our LSTM achieves ~73% accuracy using early game timeline data.
Meta shifts are detectable
Marksman ADCs declined post-2019, mage ADCs rose. The data shows real meta evolution across 12 years without any manual labeling.
ADC = most chaotic role
ADC has the highest KDA variance. Support has the lowest. High variance = player skill matters more in that role.
